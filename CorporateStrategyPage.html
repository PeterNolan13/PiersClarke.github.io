Corporate Strategy

My corporate strategy minor is where I have learned some of the most applicable skills to the workplace. With classes like fundamentals of management, marketing, accounting, corporate finance and strategy, I learned some great underlying knowledge of different business processes. In my corporate strategy class, I was tasked with coming up with a go forward business strategy for Harman Kardon, and in my marketing class I had to come up with a marketing strategy for Everly drink mix. These real world simulations gave me great insight into how companies run themselves.
