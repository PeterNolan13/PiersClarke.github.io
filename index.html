Who Am I?

I am a hardworking college student currently attending Vanderbilt University in Nashville. During the course of my academic career and over the summers, I have managed to accrue fulfilling work experience and to study abroad. This summer I had the privilege of working for Hub International in an internal consulting group as an organizational change management intern. In this role, I learned valuable professional skills such as change management assessment, leadership alignment & sponsorship and the approach to changing aspects of organizational culture. This role was particularly satisfying and developmental as I worked directly with regional leaders and their teams on major transformational changes. In my academic career and as an intern, I have been consistently praised as focused and innovative by my professors, managers and peers. Whether working on academic, extracurricular or professional projects, I apply proven problem-solving, communication and critical thinking skills to the project at hand.
